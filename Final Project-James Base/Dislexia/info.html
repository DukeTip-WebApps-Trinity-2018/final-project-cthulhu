Our Mission

Our mission is to create a space that is open, radacool and relaxing. This space will also cause human and animal interaction, which can calm both humans and animals. Our primary mission, however, is to find homes for our cats. These cats visit from the local shelter to meet people, and possibly someone to take them home.
